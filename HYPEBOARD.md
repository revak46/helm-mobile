# HypeBoard

**The mood engine.**

---

## What It Is

HypeBoard is the emotional layer of the Helm ecosystem. Six moods, curated quotes, swipe animations — a space to check in with how you're feeling and let language meet you there.

Not therapy. Not tracking. A mirror made of words.

---

## Moods

| Mood | Energy | Quote Sources |
|------|--------|--------------|
| Fired Up | High intensity | Hughes, Baldwin, Armah |
| Calm | Centered | Poe, Black Beauty |
| Reflective | Inward | Mixed, philosophical |
| Playful | Light | Mixed, cultural |
| Grateful | Warm | Mixed, spiritual |
| Determined | Driven | Hughes, Baldwin, Armah |

---

## The Experience

Swipe through quote cards with physics-based animations:

- **Slide** — horizontal momentum
- **Scale** — depth transition
- **Spring** — elastic bounce
- **Fade** — gentle dissolve

Each card is a designed moment. Typography, spacing, and animation chosen to match the mood's energy.

---

## Quote Library

Curated from voices that carry weight:

- **Langston Hughes** — dreams, resistance, beauty
- **Edgar Allan Poe** — depth, mystery, precision
- **Ayi Kwei Armah** — African philosophy, cycles
- **James Baldwin** — truth, identity, courage
- **Black Beauty** — cultural celebration
- **Mixed** — cross-tradition wisdom

In-app card creation lets you add your own.
